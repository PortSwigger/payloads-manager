Payloads Manager is a specialized Burp Suite extension designed to assist users in managing their custom payloads. This tool encourages organization and efficiency by allowing users to execute, add, edit, import, and export payloads directly within the Burp environment. This ability notably enhances the workflow speed during testing, making it an indispensable tool in an ethical hackers toolkit.

Features

  • Utilize custom payloads directly within Burp Suite.
  • Add new payloads into your testing arsenal fast and trouble-free.
  • Edit existing payloads according to your specific requirements.
  • Effortlessly import and export payloads to share with your team or for backup purposes.

Usage

Access the extension via a new \Payloads Manager\ tab available within the Burp Suite platform. This tab will provide options where you can:

  • Add new custom payloads for your future testing.
  • Edit existing payloads → achieve a better fit for each unique testing environment.
  • Import payloads → enable the sharing of custom payloads among your team.
  • Export payloads → allow for easy backup or distribution of your custom payloads.
